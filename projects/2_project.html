SOBAR

a soft aerial robot for "impact-based fast perching"

SOBAR is soft aerial robot made of compliant arms for demonstrating the benefits of compliance in aerial-physical interaction. We show how compliance is the key to perform fast perching even in presence of obstacles and on various objects. (Nguyen et al., 2023)
